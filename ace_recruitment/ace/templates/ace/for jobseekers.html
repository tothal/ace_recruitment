Writing your CV
Everything starts with your CV, therefore when writing it you have to be extra careful. We offer you some useful tips:


This is how we insure that any employers can open and read the file easily.

Up to date and stated at the beginning so the employer can get in contact with you easily.

Try not to include irrelevant information for the job you apply. Your CV needs to be easy to read.

It’s important not to make spelling or grammar mistakes.
